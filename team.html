INTERESTED IN JOINING OUR TEAM?

The best games are created by happy and fulfilled teams. For us, that recipe is fair compensation, an agile work environment and giving back our time. We spend 10% of our time giving back to charities or the indie game development community.

Our team is based in the beautiful Orange County, California. Although we love collaborating and building relationships with the indie game development community all over the world, we strive for our full time team members to live in the greater Southern California area.
